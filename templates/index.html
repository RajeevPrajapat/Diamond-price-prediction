About Diamonds

Diamonds are evaluated based on several key attributes. Here’s a breakdown:

Attribute Description
Carat The weight of the diamond.
Cut Quality of the diamond’s cut, affecting its brilliance.
Color Diamond color ranges from D (colorless) to J (slightly colored).
Clarity Measures the presence of internal or external flaws.
Depth Height of the diamond in relation to its width.
Table The width of the diamond’s top surface.
Dimensions (X, Y, Z) Measurements of the diamond’s length, width, and depth.
